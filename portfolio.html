Energy Storage with Li-Ion Battery Banks

Li-Ion technology offers high energy density, longer cycle life, and rapid charging capabilities. Deployed in various scales, from residential to industrial applications, these battery banks contribute to a more resilient and sustainable energy infrastructure by storing excess energy during low demand periods and releasing it during peak demand or grid disruptions.

EV Charging Stations – 2W & 4W

For 2-wheelers, compact Level 1 and Level 2 charging stations offer convenient and swift charging, promoting urban mobility. Meanwhile, 4-wheelers utilize a range of charging options, from Level 2 chargers for daily use to high-powered DC Fast Charging stations strategically located along highways, enabling efficient long-distance travel.
